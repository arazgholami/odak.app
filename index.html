Welcome to Odak.app

Odak.app is a simple, clean, and distraction-free writing app designed to help your thoughts flow without interruption—no ads, no servers, no clutter. Everything is stored locally on your device, and you can save, edit, and download your writings as Markdown files.

You can find a complete guide to Markdown formatting in the Manual, accessible from the left toolbar.
